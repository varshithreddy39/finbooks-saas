How it Works

Up and running in minutes

1

Set up your company profile

Enter your GSTIN (we'll verify it instantly), business name, address, bank details, and upload your logo. Takes under 3 minutes.

2

Add customers, vendors & products

Build your master data once. Customer GSTIN, state, HSN codes — all auto-filled every time you create a new invoice or quotation.

3

Create & send invoices

Select customer, add products, CGST/SGST auto-calculates. Preview PDF, send via email, or download — all in under 60 seconds.

4

Track payments & GST

Dashboard shows paid, unpaid, overdue. GST reports show monthly CGST/SGST/IGST breakdown and net payable — ready for your CA.
